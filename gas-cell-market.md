<p><ul><li>Open</li><li>Sealed</li><li>Vapor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas cell markets are categorized into three main types: open, sealed, and vapor. </p><p>Open gas cells allow for direct gas exchange with the environment, enabling real-time analysis but may compromise accuracy. Sealed gas cells are hermetically closed, preventing gas exchange and ensuring stable internal conditions, making them ideal for precise measurements. Vapor gas cells focus on vapor analysis, leveraging specific gas-vapor interactions for applications in sensors and spectroscopy. Each type serves distinct analytical needs across various industries, from environmental monitoring to industrial processing.</p></p>
