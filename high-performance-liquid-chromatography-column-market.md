<p><p>The high-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC) column market is primarily divided into HPLC columns and ultra-high-performance liquid chromatography (UHPLC) columns. HPLC columns are designed for traditional liquid chromatography applications, providing reliable separation of compounds. In contrast, UHPLC columns operate under higher pressures and utilize smaller particle sizes, enhancing resolution and speed. These advancements lead to improved sensitivity and analysis time, making UHPLC increasingly popular in pharmaceutical, environmental, and biochemical research applications, driving growth in the overall chromatography market.</p></p>
