<p><p>Hot stamper market types include manual, electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic systems. Manual hot stampers rely on human operation to apply heat and pressure for marking, making them cost-effective and easy to use for small batches. Electric models offer precision and automation, suitable for higher production rates. Hydraulic systems deliver high force and are ideal for heavy materials, while pneumatic options utilize compressed air for efficient operation, typically in medium to high volume applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
