<p><strong>The Digital Temperature Controller-Limiters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Controller</li><li>Proportional Controller</li><li>Pid Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital temperature controller-limiters market encompasses three primary types: switch controllers, proportional controllers, and PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) controllers. Switch controllers provide basic on/off functionality, ensuring temperatures stay within set limits. Proportional controllers adjust the heating or cooling output based on the temperature difference, offering more gradual control. PID controllers enhance this by integrating past and future temperature behaviors, delivering precise temperature regulation. Each type caters to varying application needs, from simple appliances to complex industrial systems.</p></p>
