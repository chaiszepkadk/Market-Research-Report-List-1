<p><p>The Bunion Correction System market is categorized into two primary types: Embedded Type and Wearable Type. Embedded Type devices involve surgical interventions where corrective implants or screws are integrated within the foot structure to realign the affected bones. In contrast, Wearable Type devices are designed for non-invasive treatment, allowing users to wear braces or orthotic devices that apply pressure to gradually reposition the bunion. Both types aim to alleviate pain, enhance mobility, and improve overall foot health.</p></p>
