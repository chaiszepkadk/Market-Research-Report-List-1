<p><p>The reversing camera market primarily consists of two types of image sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras offer superior image quality with low noise and are often preferred for high-end applications, due to their excellent light sensitivity. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more cost-effective and consume less power, making them popular for widely used reversing systems. Each technology caters to different consumer needs, driving growth in various automotive applications.</p></p>
