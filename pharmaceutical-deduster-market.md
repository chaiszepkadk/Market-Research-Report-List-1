<p><ul><li>Bag Filter</li><li>Elevating Dedusting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical deduster market comprises various types, primarily focusing on bag filters and elevating dedusters. Bag filters utilize fabric filtration to remove fine powders and contaminants during production processes, ensuring product purity. Elevating dedusters, on the other hand, incorporate a vertical transport mechanism that separates dust and particles from tablets or capsules as they move, enhancing the efficiency of the cleaning process. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining the quality and safety of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
