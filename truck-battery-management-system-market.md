<p><p>The Truck Battery Management System market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid cooling and heating systems and air cooling and heating systems. Liquid cooling systems use coolant fluids to regulate battery temperatures, enhancing efficiency and performance, particularly in heavy-duty applications. In contrast, air cooling systems rely on airflow to manage temperature, offering a simpler and often less expensive solution. Both systems aim to optimize battery lifespan and performance, catering to various operational requirements in the trucking industry.</p></p>
