<p><p>The Electronics Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market comprises three primary types. The **Unit Load Type** AGVs handle large, palletized loads, efficiently transporting goods within warehouses and production floors. **Automated Forklift Type** AGVs resemble traditional forklifts but operate independently for loading and unloading tasks, enhancing flexibility in material handling. Lastly, **Tugger Type** AGVs are designed to pull trailers or carts, facilitating the movement of multiple loads simultaneously, making them ideal for streamlined workflows in manufacturing and distribution environments.</p></p>
