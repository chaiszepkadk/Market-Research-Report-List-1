<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water Reactor Nuclear Power Plant</li><li>Boiling Water Reactor Nuclear Power Plant</li><li>Heavy Water Reactor Nuclear Power Plant</li><li>Fast Reactor Nuclear Power Plant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power plant market is categorized into several types based on design and coolant used. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWRs) utilize pressurized water to transfer heat and produce steam. Boiling Water Reactors (BWRs) allow water to boil directly in the reactor core, generating steam. Heavy Water Reactors (HWRs) use heavy water as both a moderator and coolant, facilitating the use of natural uranium fuel. Fast Reactors (FRs) operate with fast neutrons and are designed for breeding fuel, enhancing energy sustainability.</p></p>
