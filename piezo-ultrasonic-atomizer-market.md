<p><ul><li>Wide Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers</li><li>Narrow Spray Ultrasonic Atomizers</li><li>Radial Flow Ultrasonic Atomizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo ultrasonic atomizer market includes three main types: wide spray ultrasonic atomizers, narrow spray ultrasonic atomizers, and radial flow ultrasonic atomizers. Wide spray atomizers distribute aerosol particles over a larger area, making them suitable for applications like humidification and agriculture. Narrow spray atomizers produce a focused mist for precise applications, such as in medical or industrial uses. Radial flow atomizers create a circular spray pattern, enhancing efficiency in dispersing liquids evenly across surfaces, ideal for various industrial processes.</p></p>
