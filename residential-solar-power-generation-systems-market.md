<p><p>The residential solar power generation market consists of two main types: centralized and decentralized PV power plants. Centralized PV power plants are large-scale installations that generate electricity at a single location and distribute it through the grid, benefiting from economies of scale and optimized production. In contrast, decentralized PV power plants are smaller systems installed directly on residential properties, allowing homeowners to generate their own electricity, reduce energy bills, and enhance energy independence while contributing to local renewable energy production.</p></p>
