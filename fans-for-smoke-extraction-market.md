<p><ul><li>Axial Smoke Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Smoke Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke extraction fan market primarily comprises axial smoke fans and centrifugal smoke fans, each serving essential roles in fire safety systems. Axial smoke fans utilize a propeller-like design to move air in a straight line, making them effective for low-pressure applications over long distances. In contrast, centrifugal smoke fans generate high pressure through a rotating impeller, suitable for controlling smoke in larger spaces or during intense fire scenarios. Both types are crucial for ensuring safe environment evacuation during emergencies.</p></p>
