<p><p>Accident recorders are devices that capture and store data related to vehicle incidents. The market is primarily categorized into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel recorders focus on capturing data from one source, typically monitoring critical parameters like speed or braking. In contrast, dual-channel recorders provide a more comprehensive view by simultaneously recording data from two sources, such as vehicle dynamics and driver behavior. This added functionality helps improve safety analysis and accident investigation, making dual-channel models increasingly popular.</p></p>
