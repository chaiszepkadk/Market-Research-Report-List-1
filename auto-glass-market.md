<p><p>The auto glass market comprises various types, primarily including laminated glass, tempered glass, and other specialized options. Laminated glass features a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) between two glass sheets, enhancing safety and reducing shattering. Tempered glass, on the other hand, undergoes thermal treatment, making it stronger and less likely to break into sharp shards upon impact. Other market types may include polycarbonate and heated glass, catering to specific vehicle requirements and improving performance, safety, and passenger comfort.</p></p>
