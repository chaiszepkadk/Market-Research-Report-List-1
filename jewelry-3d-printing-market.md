<p><strong>The Jewelry 3D Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Fused Depositing Modelling (FDM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jewelry 3D Printing market encompasses several technologies, each with distinct characteristics. Stereolithography (SLA) uses UV light to cure resin, producing high-detail prints ideal for intricate designs. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) employs a laser to fuse powdered materials, suitable for creating complex geometries with strong durability. Digital Light Processing (DLP) also uses light to resin cure but functions faster than SLA. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic layers, offering affordability but lower resolution. Other methods include binder jetting and metal 3D printing.</p></p>
