<p><strong>The PCB Drilling and Milling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Drilling Machines</li><li>Mechanical Drilling Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB drilling and milling machine market is primarily divided into two types: laser drilling machines and mechanical drilling machines. Laser drilling machines utilize focused laser beams to create precise holes in printed circuit boards, offering high accuracy and the ability to work with delicate materials. In contrast, mechanical drilling machines use drill bits to physically remove material, providing cost-effective solutions for larger production runs. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, balancing precision and efficiency in PCB production.</p></p>
