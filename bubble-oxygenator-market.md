<p><p>The Bubble Oxygenator market comprises two main types: Hard Shell Oxygenators and Bag Type Oxygenators. Hard Shell Oxygenators feature a rigid design that provides stability and efficient gas exchange, ideal for long-term applications in critical care settings. In contrast, Bag Type Oxygenators are more flexible, allowing for easier handling and transport, often used in emergency situations or less complex procedures. Both types play crucial roles in providing oxygenation during cardiopulmonary bypass surgeries and other medical interventions.</p></p>
