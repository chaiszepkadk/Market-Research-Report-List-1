<p><p>Adhesive dispersions encompass various types, including Styrene/Acrylate dispersions, VAE (Vinyl Acetate Ethylene) dispersions, and SBL (Styrene Butadiene Latex). Styrene/Acrylate dispersions are versatile and provide strong adhesion and flexibility, commonly used in coatings and adhesives. VAE dispersions offer excellent water resistance and are favored in construction and woodworking. SBL, known for its high elasticity and adhesion, is primarily utilized in pressure-sensitive adhesives. Each type caters to specific applications based on performance and environmental requirements.</p></p>
