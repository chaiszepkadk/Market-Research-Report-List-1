<p><p>Tilting disk check valves are categorized by their connection types, including flanged end, threaded end, and welding end. Flanged end valves feature flat surfaces with bolt holes for secure connections, suitable for high-pressure applications. Threaded end valves use screw threads for easy installation and removal, ideal for smaller systems. Welding end valves are permanently attached through welding, providing a robust and leak-free solution, often used in high-temperature or high-pressure environments. Each type serves specific application needs and installation preferences.</p></p>
