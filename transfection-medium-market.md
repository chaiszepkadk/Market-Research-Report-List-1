<p><p>The transfection medium market is categorized into two primary types: liquid transfection medium and dry powder transfection medium. Liquid transfection medium is ready-to-use and facilitates the efficient delivery of nucleic acids into cells, often preferred for its convenience and immediate application. In contrast, dry powder transfection medium requires reconstitution before use, offering prolonged shelf life and stability. Both types are essential in research and therapeutic applications, catering to diverse user needs in molecular biology, gene therapy, and biotechnology.</p></p>
