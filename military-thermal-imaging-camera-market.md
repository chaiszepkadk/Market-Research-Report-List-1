<p><p>The military thermal imaging camera market is categorized into two main types: passive and active systems. Passive thermal imaging cameras detect infrared radiation emitted naturally by objects, providing a stealthy surveillance capability without any external light source. In contrast, active thermal imaging systems project their own infrared light, illuminating the environment and enhancing visibility in dark conditions. Both types play crucial roles in military operations, offering unique advantages in reconnaissance, target acquisition, and situational awareness.</p></p>
