<p><p>The Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) testing market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes. Sandwich ELISA is used for detecting antigens between two antibodies, ensuring high specificity. Indirect ELISA measures antibodies in a sample, facilitating infection diagnosis. Multiple and portable ELISA formats allow simultaneous testing and convenience in field settings. Competitive ELISA, on the other hand, quantifies analytes by competition, often used for small molecules. Together, these formats enhance diagnostic capabilities across diverse applications.</p></p>
