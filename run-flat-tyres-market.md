<p><strong>The Run-Flat Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting</li><li>Self-sealing</li><li>Auxiliary-supported</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Run-flat tyres are designed to maintain functionality after a puncture, categorized into three types. Self-supporting tyres feature reinforced sidewalls that allow them to support the vehicle's weight even when deflated. Self-sealing tyres contain an internal sealant that automatically fills punctures, preventing air loss. Auxiliary-supported tyres utilize an additional support ring to maintain structural integrity after deflation. Each type offers unique advantages, enhancing safety and convenience for drivers by allowing continued mobility despite tyre damage.</p></p>
