<p><p>The PEEK gear market consists of various types, each serving distinct mechanical applications. Spur gears, characterized by their straight teeth, are ideal for transmitting power between parallel shafts. Helical gears, with their angled teeth, provide smoother and quieter operation, making them suitable for higher speed applications. Bevel gears, featuring conical shapes, enable the transfer of motion between non-parallel shafts. Additionally, the "other" category includes specialized gear types like worm gears and planetary gears, catering to unique industrial needs.</p></p>
