<p><p>The barrel pumps market comprises various types designed for fluid transfer. Siphon pumps utilize gravity to move liquids, making them simple and effective for low-viscosity fluids. Rotary pumps use mechanical action to dispense liquids, ideal for high-viscosity substances. Hand pumps are manually operated, providing portability for small tasks or emergency situations. Piston pumps use reciprocating motion to deliver precise volumes, suitable for high-pressure applications. Each type caters to specific needs within industries like chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
