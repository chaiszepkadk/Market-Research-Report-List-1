<p><p>Concrete volute pumps are categorized into single-stage and multistage designs, serving various applications in construction and industrial sectors. Single-stage volute pumps are characterized by a single impeller, delivering moderate flow rates and pressure for typical tasks like drainage. In contrast, multistage volute pumps incorporate multiple impellers, enabling higher pressure and flow capabilities, suitable for demanding applications such as elevating water in large infrastructures. These distinctions cater to specific operational needs and efficiency requirements in different environments.</p></p>
