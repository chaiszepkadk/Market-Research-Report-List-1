<p><p>The modular cleanroom market is divided into two primary types: modular softwall and modular hardwall cleanrooms. Modular softwall cleanrooms are flexible structures featuring fabric or plastic walls, offering easy setup and reconfiguration for varying needs, often used in less stringent environments. In contrast, modular hardwall cleanrooms are built with rigid, durable materials providing superior contamination control, ideal for industries requiring strict compliance, such as pharmaceuticals and electronics. Both types facilitate scalable and customizable cleanroom solutions for various applications.</p></p>
