<p><p>The SCR denitration catalyst market encompasses three main types: honeycomb, plate, and corrugated. Honeycomb catalysts feature a channel structure that promotes efficient gas flow and high surface area for reactions. Plate-type catalysts provide compact designs ideal for limited spaces, optimizing performance in smaller systems. Corrugated catalysts create turbulence in the gas flow, enhancing contact between gases and the catalyst, thus improving reaction efficiency. Each type serves specific applications based on spatial constraints and performance needs in denitration processes.</p></p>
