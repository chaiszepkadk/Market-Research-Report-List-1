<p><p>The periodontal probes market encompasses two main types: single-ended and double-ended probes. Single-ended probes feature a single measurement tip, making them ideal for focused assessments in tight spaces or specific areas. Both types are crucial for periodontal assessments, helping clinicians evaluate gum health, pocket depth, and overall periodontal conditions efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
