<p><p>The Raman-AFM market encompasses two primary technologies: Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) and Raman spectroscopy. AFM provides high-resolution imaging and surface topography analysis at the nanoscale by measuring force interactions between a cantilever and a sample. In contrast, Raman spectroscopy analyzes molecular vibrations to provide chemical composition information via scattered light. Together, these techniques enhance material characterization, offering insights into physical and chemical properties, driving applications in nanotechnology, materials science, life sciences, and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
