<p><p>The cardiovascular agents market comprises various categories targeting heart-related conditions. Antianginal agents alleviate chest pain by improving blood flow. Antiarrhythmic agents regulate heart rhythm disorders. Inotropic agents enhance heart muscle contractility, while miscellaneous cardiovascular agents cover diverse treatments. Peripheral vasodilators improve circulation by widening blood vessels, and renin inhibitors block hypertension pathways. Sclerosing agents induce scar tissue formation to treat varicose veins, whereas vasodilators expand blood vessels to lower blood pressure, collectively contributing to cardiovascular health management.</p></p>
