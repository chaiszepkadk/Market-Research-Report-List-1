<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drying tunnel market consists of two primary types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal drying tunnels feature a conveyor system that allows products to move linearly through a heated space, promoting efficient drying for bulk goods. In contrast, vertical drying tunnels utilize a stacked arrangement, optimizing floor space while allowing gravity to assist in the drying process. Both designs cater to specific production needs, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in various industrial applications, including food processing, textiles, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
