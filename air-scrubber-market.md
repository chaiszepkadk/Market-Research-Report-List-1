<p><p>The air scrubber market comprises two primary types: wet air scrubbers and dry air scrubbers. Wet air scrubbers utilize a liquid solution to capture and remove pollutants from the air, effectively eliminating particulate matter and harmful gases. In contrast, dry air scrubbers employ adsorbent materials to trap contaminants without the need for water, making them suitable for specific industrial applications. Both types are essential for maintaining air quality and reducing emissions in various industries, contributing to environmental sustainability.</p></p>
