<p><p>The loose powder market can be categorized into two main types: pressed type and powder type. Pressed type loose powders are compacted into a solid form, making them travel-friendly and easy to apply, often providing a matte finish. In contrast, powder type loose powders are finely milled and typically come in jars. They offer a lightweight feel and a more natural, translucent finish, allowing for buildable coverage. Both types serve to set makeup, control shine, and enhance skin texture, catering to diverse preferences.</p></p>
