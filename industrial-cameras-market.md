<p><strong>The Industrial Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cameras market is primarily divided into area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture complete images in a single exposure, making them ideal for stationary applications where the entire scene is visible. They are commonly used in quality control and inspection tasks. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, suitable for moving objects or continuous production lines. They excel in high-speed applications like web inspection and large-area imaging.</p></p>
