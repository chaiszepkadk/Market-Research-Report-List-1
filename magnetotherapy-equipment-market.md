<p><strong>The Magnetotherapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Energy Magnets</li><li>Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetotherapy equipment encompasses two main types: Constant Energy Magnets and Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field (PEMF) devices. Constant Energy Magnets provide a steady magnetic field intended to promote healing and pain relief through consistent exposure. In contrast, PEMF devices generate fluctuating magnetic fields, which stimulate cellular processes by mimicking natural electromagnetic signals. Both types aim to enhance therapeutic effects, alleviate pain, improve circulation, and support various health conditions, making them popular options in alternative medicine and wellness sectors.</p></p>
