<p><strong>The Tile Levelling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wedge System</li><li>Screw Cap System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile levelling system market features two primary types: the wedge system and the screw cap system. The wedge system uses plastic or metal wedges inserted between tiles that lift and level them during installation, ensuring a smooth finish. Conversely, the screw cap system incorporates adjustable caps that allow for precise height adjustments, accommodating variations in tile thickness. Both systems aim to eliminate lippage and improve the overall aesthetic of tiled surfaces, catering to professional installers and DIY enthusiasts alike.</p></p>
