<p><p>Longwave Infrared (LWIR) technology is categorized into two main types: cooled and uncooled technology. Cooled LWIR systems utilize specialized cooling mechanisms to enhance sensitivity and detection capabilities, making them suitable for high-performance applications like military and scientific use. In contrast, uncooled LWIR technology operates at ambient temperatures, offering simpler designs and lower costs, making it ideal for commercial applications such as security, building temperature monitoring, and automotive systems. Both technologies serve diverse sectors with unique advantages.</p></p>
