<p><strong>The UV & Visible Spectroscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-beam Systems</li><li>Dual-beam Systems</li><li>Array-based Systems</li><li>Handheld Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV and Visible Spectroscopy market comprises several types of systems: </p><p>1. **Single-beam Systems** measure light intensity before and after passing through a sample, making them simple and cost-effective.</p><p>2. **Dual-beam Systems** compare light from two paths, enhancing accuracy and stability by simultaneously measuring reference and sample beams.</p><p>3. **Array-based Systems** use multiple detectors to capture spectral data in one shot, allowing for rapid analysis.</p><p>4. **Handheld Systems** offer portability for on-site measurements, making them ideal for fieldwork and versatile applications.</p></p>
