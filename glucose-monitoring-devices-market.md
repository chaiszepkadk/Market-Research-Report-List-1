<p><strong>The Glucose Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Monitoring Devices</li><li>Continuous Glucose Monitoring Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucose monitoring devices market comprises two main types: self-monitoring devices and continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) devices. Self-monitoring devices, typically handheld, allow individuals to measure blood glucose levels at specific times, requiring finger pricks for blood samples. In contrast, CGM devices offer real-time glucose readings through sensors placed under the skin, providing continuous and automated tracking throughout the day. Both types play a crucial role in managing diabetes and improving overall glucose control for patients.</p></p>
