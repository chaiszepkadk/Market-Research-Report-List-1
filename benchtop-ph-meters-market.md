<p><p>Benchtop pH meters are categorized into single-channel, dual-channel, and multi-channel types based on their measurement capabilities. Single-channel meters are designed for straightforward applications, measuring one sample at a time. Dual-channel meters allow simultaneous monitoring of two samples, enhancing efficiency in comparative studies. Multi-channel meters can analyze multiple samples concurrently, making them ideal for extensive laboratory work. Each type caters to different laboratory needs, balancing complexity, throughput, and user requirements in various research and quality control applications.</p></p>
