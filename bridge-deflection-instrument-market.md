<p><ul><li>Dynamic Bridge Deflection Instrument</li><li>Static Bridge Deflection Instrument</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bridge Deflection Instrument market is divided into two main types: Dynamic and Static Bridge Deflection Instruments. Dynamic Bridge Deflection Instruments are designed to measure real-time deflection caused by traffic loads or environmental factors, providing insights into structural performance during varying conditions. In contrast, Static Bridge Deflection Instruments focus on long-term monitoring of bridge stability, measuring deflections under static loads without the influence of moving vehicles. Both instruments are essential for ensuring bridge safety and integrity in civil engineering projects.</p></p>
