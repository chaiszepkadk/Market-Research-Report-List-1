<p><ul><li>Optical Fingerprint Film</li><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint films market is primarily divided into two types: optical fingerprint films and capacitive fingerprint films. Optical fingerprint films use light to capture finger patterns, making them ideal for high-resolution applications. In contrast, capacitive fingerprint films rely on electric charge differences to detect ridges and grooves in fingerprints, offering faster response times and durability. Both types cater to various industries, including security, personal identification, and access control, enhancing biometric authentication solutions across multiple platforms.</p></p>
