<p><strong>The Temperature Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On/Off Control</li><li>Proportional Control</li><li>PID Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature controller market is segmented into three main types: On/Off Control, Proportional Control, and PID Control. On/Off Control provides a simple, binary response to temperature changes, turning heating or cooling devices on or off. Proportional Control adjusts the output in relation to the temperature difference, allowing for smoother operation. PID Control combines Proportional, Integral, and Derivative actions to minimize error and improve stability, making it suitable for complex processes requiring precise temperature regulation. Each type addresses unique application needs in various industries.</p></p>
